About Me

Hello, my name is Jacob Chatfield and I'm a student web-developer from Surrey, England. I'm currently 22 years old and working as a gardener whilst I pursue a career change in an exciting and challenging field. In my spare time I enjoy socializing with friends, playing some games on my PC and playing sports, mainly football. I used to play for a local Sunday league team until this bootcamp took up all my spare time! I couldn't complain though, I'm really enjoying the challenge and the idea of up-skilling myself.
When I was about 13/14, I took an interest in programming and web development and I took a little basic course at Codecademy completing their Javascript, HTML and CSS courses. Although I struggled at parts, the idea of programming and problem solving as a career really interested me, but unfortunately I didn't do much from there. Fast forward 10 or so years and I've decided I want to get back into it again. The idea of a challenging career with a satisfactory pay-off really appeals to me, and I thought the best way forward was for me to get my foot in the door as efficiently as I possibly could by studying independantly and taking some online courses.
